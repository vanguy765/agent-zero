### Key Design Decision
Used **partial unique indexes** instead of `COALESCE` with sentinel UUIDs for both `project_outcomes` and `type_orders` — this is the PostgreSQL-idiomatic way to handle unique constraints with nullable columns:
```sql
CREATE UNIQUE INDEX idx_..._unique_system ON table(code) WHERE scope = 'system';
CREATE UNIQUE INDEX idx_..._unique_company ON table(code, organization_id) WHERE scope = 'company';
```
