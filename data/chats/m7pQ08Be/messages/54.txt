147:### 🏥 Availability Guards (Extension)
153:    """Availability Guard: verify the API is reachable before starting work."""
165:Availability Guards are an extension — useful in practice, but not required for Bulwark compliance. See the [Guide](GUIDE.md#52-availability-guards) for details.
299:Guards are O(1) type checks — nanoseconds. Traces log a shape string — microseconds. Verifies may add one lightweight query. With v0.2.0's verify strategies, you can sample or adapt verification for high-throughput systems. Unmeasurable for 99% of applications.
