## Important Patterns

- The linter detects `guard_*()`, `trace_*()`, `verify_*()` naming conventions via regex
- Custom error types: `GuardError`, `VerifyError` (raise, never assert)
- Use `if not condition: raise GuardError(...)` — never bare `assert`
- All traces should include structured logging with context (operation_id, etc.)
